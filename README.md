# Phantom Racing Team Website - 2025 Season
This repository contains the official website for Phantom Racing. We are a team based in Redmond, Washington and are actively participating in the STEM Racing Competition. The website serves as a platform for sponsors to contact the team and showcase our work to fans and friends alike. 
## Purpose and Audience
The purpose of this website is to demonstrate pictures, events, and other processes that help our team function and will showcase the project management throughout the year. 

### Primary audiences include: 
- **Sponsors**: To demonstate professionalism of Phantom Racing and the development of our car and other project deliverables
- **College Admission Officers**: To showcase our technical skills and leadership for the workforce
- **Fans and Family**: Allows them to follow our progress and tells future events
