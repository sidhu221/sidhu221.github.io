About Phantom Racing

We are a dedicated racing team committed to excellence on and off the track. With cutting-edge technology, skilled drivers, and a passionate crew, Phantom Racing pushes the boundaries of speed and performance.

Our Team

Meet the talented individuals who make Phantom Racing a force to be reckoned with.

Tharan Sivakumar
Team Principal

Tharan is a junior at Redmond High School who is extremely passionate about Formula 1. Ever since he was a young child, he always loved watching Formula 1 and he also likes engineering a lot, so Tharan founded Phantom Racing to compete at the highest level of motorsport.

Sidhanth Chakravarthy
Portfolio Lead

Sidhanth is a junior at Redmond High School and is the Portfolio Lead for Phantom Racing. He oversees the branding, documentation, and presentation of Phantom Racing to the judges of STEM Racing. He has a background in VEX robotics as the engineer notebooker where they won many awards.

Sharav Dhar
Chief Engineer

Sharav is a junior at Redmond High School who is passionate about engineering and motorsports. He has a keen interest in aerodynamics and vehicle design, which he has pursued through various projects and competitions. Sharav is excited to contribute his skills and knowledge to the Phantom Racing team as they compete in the STEM Racing competition.

Rishab Nidadavolu
Lead Design Engineer

Rising star ready to take on any challenge on the track.

Jash Vohra
Lead Finance Manager

Jash is a junior at Redmond High School who is extremely excited to be a part of Phantom Racing. He has a strong passion for motorsports and engineering, and he enjoys working on various projects related to cars and racing. Jash is looking forward to contributing his skills and knowledge to help the team succeed in the STEM Racing competition. Jash also runs his own non-profit organization called LUMA Coding, hosting several hackathons with huge cash prizes of $1000+ as well as teaching thousands of children each year the skills they need to excel in computer science. This showcases his leadership skills as well as his extremely diligent work ethic.

Guna Sen
Chief of Marketing

Uses advanced telemetry to optimize our race strategies.

Kandarp Vadadoriya
Marketing Coordinator

Kandarp is a junior at Redmond High School who is thrilled to be part of Phantom Racing. He has a deep passion for motorsports and engineering, and enjoys working on various projects related to cars and racing. Kandarp is eager to contribute his skills and knowledge to help the team excel in the F1 in Schools competition.

Arnav Jha
Social Media Lead

Arnav is a junior at Redmond High School passionate about engineering and motorsports. From a young age, he has enjoyed programming and robotics through competitions like USACO and VEX Robotics. He loves designing and building high performance vehicles and is excited to contribute his skills to the Phantom Racing team. Outside of Formula 1, Arnav competitively swims for Redmond High School and a club team, earning multiple awards and reaching the district level in Lake Washington Competitive Swimming. He also serves on the board of the nonprofit LUMA Coding with Jash, hosting major hackathons and teaching thousands of children computer science. His experiences highlight strong leadership and a diligent work ethic.
